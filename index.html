Mandi (formerly known as Mandav Nagar,[3][4] also known as Sahor) is a major city and a municipal corporation in Mandi District in the Indian state of Himachal Prades. It is situated 145 kilometres (90 mi) north of state capital, Shimla[5] in the north-west Himalayas at an average altitude of 880 metres [6] and experiences pleasant summers and cold winters.

Trip to Kasauli

Click here to view more photo diaries..

Kasauli is a small town in the state of Himachal Pradesh located at an altitude of 1,927 m. The town is 77 km from Shimla and 70 km from Chandigarh. Legend says that it came into existence after Lord Hanuman placed his feet here in order to advance on to the Sanjeevani hill. The place where the lord actually kept his feet is believed to be at a hillock which is 300 m high.
